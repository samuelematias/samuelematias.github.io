🇺🇸 Start your Emacs as a service on macOS startup

⭐ Featured post ⭐

To read this content in portuguese, click here .

If you want to start your Emacs as a service on the macOS startup, you need to have installed the Emacs plus , and after you have installed, execute this command:.

brew services start d12frosted/emacs-plus/emacs-plus
